### 2. Business Description

The organization designs, constructs, and manages residential housing communities. Core operations encompass the full property lifecycle: land acquisition, architectural design, construction project management, and ongoing property management for completed developments.

A significant business segment involves the development and management of residential housing for military families under contract with the Department of Defense (DoD). This contract vehicle requires compliance with federal cybersecurity standards, including DFARS 252.204-7012 and NIST SP 800-171.

### 7. Compliance Driver

The organization is contractually obligated to comply with NIST SP 800-171 as a flow-down requirement from a DoD prime contractor for military housing development and management. The prime contractor's contract includes DFARS 252.204-7012 clause, which requires the organization to:

1. Provide adequate security to protect Covered Defense Information (CDI) and CUI
2. Report cyber incidents to the DoD within 72 hours
3. Flow down security requirements to subcontractors (if applicable)

This readiness assessment is the first formal step toward documenting compliance posture and developing a remediation roadmap.
