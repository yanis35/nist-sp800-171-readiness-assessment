## 2. Project Scope

### In Scope

- All NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2 security requirements (14 families, 113 controls)
- Systems, people, and processes that handle or support CUI
- Policies, procedures, and technical controls
- Interviews with key personnel
- Technical verification of security controls
- On-premises and cloud systems within the CUI boundary

### Out of Scope

- Physical security of construction sites
- Third-party vendor systems (unless they process CUI on behalf of the organization)
- Financial audit or SOC reports
- Penetration testing (identified as a future recommendation)
- Non-CUI related corporate IT systems not connected to the CUI environment
- Supply chain risk management (SCRM) beyond CUI-handling vendors

## 8. Assumptions & Constraints

### Assumptions

- The organization has existing IT infrastructure and security policies
- Key personnel will be available for interviews
- Evidence will be provided within agreed timeframes
- No active security incident will occur during the assessment period

### Constraints

- 6-week engagement window
- Limited to internal resources; no third-party penetration testing
- Assessment is a readiness evaluation, not a formal certification audit
